ABOUT PERSPICACIOUS’18

A National Level Technical Symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science and Enginenering at Bannari Amman Institute of Technology Sathyamangalam, is a celebration of unconstrained thinking and love for the spirit of exuberant technology.

This technical fest, stretches over two days during 24th and 25th of January 2018.

Apart from the fun, frolic and platform that it offers to students to exhibit their talents, this technical symposium aims at encouraging innovative thinking among young aspirants which leads to profound technical insights.

The entire environment is planned to give a conducive atmosphere for a holistic learning.

 

Join us to experience the pageant you surely would not have savored before.

DECIDE YOUR DEFT (Idea Presentation)
“Fuel your brain to ideate the ideas that never existed“

This contest provides a competing platform where communicating about your idea becomes an art.
Provide ideas and solution to the industrial problems.

TOPICS:
1. WiFi is a widely used transmission medium in the world. Though it is a very secured transmission medium , the password of the specific Wi-Fi network can be hacked. In the recent trends , LiFi (Light Fidelity) which uses visible light as a medium transmits data with higher transmission rate than WiFi.Is it possible to design a circuit to transmit any form of data (For eg.,audio or video) from the transmission end to receiving end?
2. If you are travelling in a vehicle on a busy road. you have been distracted by the head light of the other vehicle, it may cause the accident .in order to reduce that kind of accident as a technical person what should you do?
3. In recent times it is to note that the image in the internet can be downloaded by any individual in the world. It is essential to intimate that the picture is being downloaded by other .Give some techniques to find out.
4. Nowadays most of the exams are online. Recently it is found that some people are undergoing misbehaviour activities in the form of impersonation .Help them with the solution.
5. Design a solution to solve the problem of varying image size while opening same images in different browsers.
6. Develop a system to help visually challenged people to use mobile phones efficiently for their travel without any assistance.
7. Is the present security for Cloud storage is enough or we need to enhance it?
8. Which programming language is both time and cost effective that can be used to code for android programming other than Java? Justify your answer?
9. Farmers needs to know the amount of moisture content in the soil so that it will be easier for them to supply water according to the need of the crops and physical work is required for the supply of the water... Help them out with IOT.
10. Is it possible to make the Charging of the vehicles easier ?!


RULES:
• Only 2 members per team can participate.
• Abstract of the Solution for the problem(any one) should be sent on or before "18.01.2018" to the e-mail id "deft2k18@gmail.com".
• The more feasible solution will be selected and intimated through mail on or before "19.01.2018".
• Selected participants should present the Powerpoint presentation (should not exceed 15 slides).

ROUND 1:
• The Participant should present their solution in the form of powerpoint presentation.
• Duration - 5 minutes (3 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes for query).
• The team with optimal solution will be selected for the second round.
• Judge's decisions are final.
ROUND 2:
• Problems will be provided to the shortlisted participants on the spot.
• Participants should present their solutions for which Powerpoint presentation is not necessary.
